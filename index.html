Welcome to Bongotoru!

Aami Banglay Gaan Gai – Aami Banglar Gan Gai, Aami aamar amike chiro din ei Banglay Khuje Pai…. this song by the legendary songwriter, singer and creative artist, Pratul Mukhopadhyay truly reflects the “Bangaliana” to its core. Bongotoru, the word has its meaning embedded within itself. “Bongo” means the region of Bengal and “toru” means the tree. For hundreds of Bengalis who are separated from Bengal, Bongotoru is the tree, the shadow and the conjugation string which binds and brings back the Bangaliana into the heart of Delhi-NCR and relive everything that’s “Bengali”.

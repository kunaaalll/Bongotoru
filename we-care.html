We Care

“জীবে প্রেম করে যেই জন / সেই জন সেবিছে ঈশ্বর।“
"Jibe Prem Kore Jei jon, Sei jon Sebiche Iswar" – Swamy Vivekananda
“- those who love all on this earth are truly serving to the Almighty!“

We at Bongotoru have imbibed this very though of Swami ji within ourselves and have let the essence of it permeate our body and soul. Today we are privileged to run a helping hand initiative through our “We Care” Foundation.

Through “We Care” program we arrange multiple events and camps dedicated to the people and to the social cause, for instance we organize blood donation camps, medical camps etc. Also reaching out to the underprivileged, the people who are deprived of the basic amenities and struggle to meet their daily needs, with support is a core objective of this foundation, we have been distributing winter support stuff including blankets and clothes. We organize book distribution camps, distribution of food etc.

We strongly believe in empowerment, and empowerment comes only with education, hence we are in process to set up a basic school for the “people in need” segment so that they can send their kids for basic education.

We in Bongotoru believe, Let the Love and Care propagate to the people who really need them the most.

Like Mother Teresa said… Never worry about numbers, help one person at a time and always start with the person nearest you.”
